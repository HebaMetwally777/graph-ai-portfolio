About Me

Hello! I’m Heba Metwally, a Public Health researcher with a passion for leveraging technology to accelerate discovery and improve healthcare outcomes. With experience in data‑driven analysis for decision making, I’m now diving deep into generative AI to streamline knowledge management, automate routine tasks, and communicate science more effectively.

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity

This live, 8‑week online certification focuses on No‑Code AI Tools that supercharge productivity. Key learning outcomes include:

  • Automate document, spreadsheet, and data workflows
  • Organize knowledge & conduct literature reviews with AI
  • Build custom chatbots for projects and teams
  • Create scientific visuals & presentations using AI
  • Apply skills in a hands‑on capstone project

The program is designed for students, faculty, and researchers—no coding prerequisite required—and features live workshops, help sessions, and an active support forum.
