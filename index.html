About Me

I’m Heba Metwally, a health & life sciences enthusiast leveraging AI‑driven workflows to make data‑heavy projects more insightful and efficient. With experience in research, knowledge management, and scientific writing, I’m passionate about translating complex concepts into actionable solutions.

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity is an intensive 8‑week program focused on no‑code automation. Key outcomes include:

  • Automate documentation, spreadsheets & data tasks
  • Use AI for knowledge organization & literature reviews
  • Develop custom chatbots for project or team support
  • Create professional presentations & visuals with AI
  • Apply learnings in a hands‑on capstone project

Who’s it for? Students, faculty, and researchers in health & life science fields seeking actionable AI strategies—no coding required.

Live workshops, help sessions, and forums provide real‑time support as we iterate on weekly submissions.
